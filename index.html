SpielWerkCode – Simple Games for Cognitively Impaired Users

Small games and custom jigsaw puzzles designed for people with cognitive impairments. Developed with care and simplicity.

Screenshots of SpielWerkCode games

About the Project

SpielWerkCode aims to offer simple, accessible games and interactive jigsaw puzzles for cognitively impaired users. The games feature clear interfaces, calm visuals, and easy controls, tested on various Android devices.
